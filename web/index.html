About MWCC

The Midwest Cricket Conference (MWCC) organizes multi-division competitions across the region, balancing weekend-only play with shared grounds and cross-tournament participation. Our mission is to keep scheduling fair, transparent, and accessible for clubs of every size.

Divisions

Division 1 and Division 2 feature red and white ball leagues.
